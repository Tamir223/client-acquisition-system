Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Client Machinery ("we," "us," or "our") is a done-for-you lead recovery and follow-up automation service for local service businesses, operated by Tamir Robertson, based in Greensboro, NC, USA. Our website is https://clientmachinery.com.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@clientmachinery.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a form, book a call, or become a client, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name
  • Industry / niche

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs. This information is collected through Google Analytics (see Section 5).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To set up, run, and manage your lead recovery and follow-up automation system.
  • Follow-up communications: To respond to your inquiries and follow up with you about our services.
  • Email marketing: To send you relevant updates, case studies, and offers related to Client Machinery services. You may opt out at any time.
  • Internal analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve our service.
  • Lead scoring: We use AI to evaluate the fit of leads for our service. This process is internal and does not result in any automated legal decisions affecting you.
  • Payment processing: To process subscription payments securely through Stripe.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with the third-party service providers listed below, and only to the extent necessary to operate our services:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. Your payment card information is handled directly by Stripe and never stored on our servers. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • MailerLite — Email marketing and subscriber management. Your name and email may be added to our mailing list. See MailerLite's Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram — Internal lead notifications. Lead details (name, email, business, industry) are sent to our private business Telegram channel for operational purposes only.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-powered lead scoring. Submitted lead data (name, company, industry) may be processed by Anthropic's API to generate a fit score. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and visitor behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and report on how users interact with our site. The data collected is anonymized and aggregated.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies primarily for analytics (Google Analytics). Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. If you'd like us to delete your data, please contact us at support@clientmachinery.com.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@clientmachinery.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Client Portal

Paying clients are provided access to a private client portal at clientmachinery.com/portal. When you log in and use the portal, we collect the following data:

  • Email address — used to authenticate your account
  • Business name — displayed within your portal session
  • Uploaded lead data — including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and service details submitted via CSV or manual entry

Lead data uploaded through the portal is stored securely in our database and is used solely to run the automated follow-up sequences included in your subscription. It is not sold, shared with third parties for marketing purposes, or used for any purpose beyond delivering the service you have subscribed to.

Portal access is tied to your active subscription. Upon cancellation, portal access is terminated and your uploaded lead data is retained for 30 days before deletion, unless you request earlier removal.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
