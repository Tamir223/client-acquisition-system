1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to operate the Client Machinery service:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and business name provided when signing up
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe; we never store, see, or have access to your card details
  • Lead data — names, email addresses, and phone numbers of the prospects you upload to your portal
  • Gmail OAuth tokens — access and refresh tokens stored securely and used exclusively to send follow-up emails on your behalf
  • Telegram chat ID — stored if you connect Telegram, used only to deliver notifications about your own leads
  • Usage data — leads uploaded, emails sent, email open events, and portal activity used to generate your performance reports
  • Google Sheet data — lead records written to a Google Sheet created and managed on your behalf in your own Google account

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide the Client Machinery service
  • To send automated follow-up emails on your behalf using your connected Gmail
  • To send you Telegram notifications when your leads reply or book
  • To generate weekly performance reports delivered to your email
  • To detect and suppress unsubscribe requests from your leads
  • To improve reliability and performance of our platform

We never sell your data to third parties. We never use your lead data for our own marketing or share it with other clients.

3. Your Leads Data

Lead data you upload belongs entirely to you. We process it solely to operate the follow-up sequences you configure.

  • Lead data is stored in your personal Google Sheet, which you own and control independently of Client Machinery
  • We do not share your leads with other clients or any third parties beyond what is necessary to deliver emails
  • Each client's data is completely isolated — no other client can access your leads
  • You can export or delete your lead data at any time from your portal or by contacting support

4. Gmail Integration

Connecting your Gmail is optional. When you do:

  • We request permission to send emails and read replies in your Gmail inbox
  • We only access emails directly relevant to your Client Machinery follow-up sequences
  • We never read personal emails unrelated to your sequences
  • OAuth tokens are stored encrypted and never shared
  • You can disconnect Gmail at any time from your portal settings — this immediately stops all email sending
  • You can also revoke access directly from your Google Account security settings

When Gmail is not connected, emails are sent from support@clientmachinery.com via our transactional email provider until you connect your own address.

5. Telegram Integration

Connecting Telegram is optional. When you do:

  • We store your Telegram chat ID to deliver notifications about your leads and sequences
  • We only send notifications about activity in your own account
  • We never send unsolicited marketing messages via Telegram
  • You can disconnect Telegram at any time from your portal settings

6. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using the bcrypt algorithm — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Sessions are authenticated using short-lived JWT tokens stored in your browser's session storage, not cookies
  • Google service account credentials and OAuth secrets are stored as encrypted environment variables, not in the database
  • Database access is restricted to the application server only
  • We never store Stripe payment card details — payments are handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure

While we apply industry-standard security practices, no system is perfectly secure. We recommend using a strong unique password for your account.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your subscription is active
  • After cancellation, account data is retained for 30 days before deletion
  • You may request immediate deletion by emailing support@clientmachinery.com
  • Lead data in your Google Sheet remains in your Google account and is governed by Google's own retention policies — it is not deleted when you cancel Client Machinery

8. Third Party Services

Client Machinery relies on the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Stripe — payment processing  stripe.com/privacy
  • Google — Gmail, Sheets, Drive  policies.google.com
  • Resend — transactional email delivery  resend.com/privacy
  • Telegram — push notifications  telegram.org/privacy
  • Render — cloud hosting  render.com/privacy

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your lead data from your portal at any time
  • Disconnect Gmail or Telegram integrations at any time from your portal settings
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@clientmachinery.com. We will respond within 7 business days.

10. Cookies

We use minimal browser storage:

  • Session storage — used to hold your authentication token for the duration of your browser session; cleared when you close the tab
  • No tracking cookies
  • No advertising cookies
  • No third-party analytics

11. Children

Client Machinery is a business service not directed at individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will notify clients of material changes to this policy via email to their registered address at least 7 days before the changes take effect. The updated policy will also be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
